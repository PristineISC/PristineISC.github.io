Maharjan Ul Barakah

Pristine Private School is thrilled to host its first-ever Maharjan Ul Barakah on February 5, 2025! This event aims to inspire and enlighten young hearts and minds through the words of Allah (SWT), encouraging them to seek the guidance of those who lead them towards righteousness.

Details

Best Muazzin(Azaan-Call to prayer):

               Boys only

               2 KS3 and 2 KS4&5 participants per school

               1-4 minutes per participant

               Participants will be judged on:

                              -Memory

                              -Sound and emotions

                              -Mahraj(Pronunciation & Tune)

                              -Makamath(expression)

                              -Time Management

               Time & Place:

                              -8:50 am to 10:30 am

                              -MPH


Best Muthalib(Nasheed Recitation):

               Girls only

               1 KS3 and 2 KS4&5 participants per school

               2-4 minutes per participant

               Arabic or English nasheed

               Participants will be judged on:

                              -Voice Quality- [vocal intonation and projection, accuracy of pitch, tone colour]

                              -Diction- [colour of vowels, proper emphasis, or accent on the words]

                              -Timing- [softness and loudness, rhythm]

                              -Interpretation & Stage Presence- [facial expression, proper focus]

               Time & Place:

                              8:50 am to 10:30 am

                              Class 12C



Best Presentation:

               Boys & Girls

               Group of 3 (1 group per school)

               5 minutes per presentation

               Theme:

                              -How the UAE 2031 Initiative (Forward Society, Forward Economy, Forward Diplomacy and Forward Ecosystem) connects to Islam

               Participants will be judged on:

                              -Organization
                              -Content
                              -Time management
                              -Dialogue

               Time & Place:

                              11:00 am to 12:20 pm


Rules & Regulations

Registration should be done through the provided link, and must be duly submitted by Monday, 30 January 2025.


1. Adhere strictly to the time limits specified for each competition. A penalty will be applied for exceeding the allocated time. A cautionary bell will ring 15 seconds before the designated time concludes. A prolonged bell will signal the end of the allotted time, followed by a warning bell 15 seconds later. Participants who fall short of or exceed the prescribed time limit will face penalties.

2. The decision of the judges is final and binding. Participants must respect the outcomes and avoid disputes.

3.The team's total marks will be the basis for calculation. The school that secures the highest points will be honoured with a certificate and a trophy.

4. An exceptional award will be granted to the overall best Muzzain and Nasheed reciter.

5.Certificates and awards will be presented for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd places individually.

6. Registration completion should be done through the provided link, separately for both girls and boys, and must be duly submitted by Friday, 31st January 2025
